**Block 3 — Card stagger (ANIM-03, per D-03, D-04):**
All cards in `.card-grid` get the fade-in animation. First 6 cards get staggered `animation-delay` at 75ms intervals. Cards 7+ default to 0ms delay (no explicit rule needed — base rule has no delay).
```css
.card-grid .post-card {
```

Replace:
