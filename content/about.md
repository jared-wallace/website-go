# About

Jared Wallace is a software engineer who builds things for the web and occasionally for himself.
He lives somewhere between the keyboard and the ocean, mostly at the keyboard.

## The Wild Meridian

This blog exists because writing is how ideas stop being vague and start being useful.
The name comes from the idea of a point you navigate toward — somewhere out past the known chart.
Dispatches from the deep end, as it were.

## What You'll Find Here

Technical writing, occasional observations, and the odd deep-dive into something that seemed simple until it wasn't.
No particular schedule, no particular niche — just things worth putting down.
